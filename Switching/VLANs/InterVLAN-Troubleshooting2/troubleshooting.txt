Lab Title: Inter-VLAN Routing Troubleshooting

Objective:
Ensure PC1 can communicate with all other PCs (PC2, PC3, PC4) across VLANs 13 and 24.

Network Summary:
- VLAN 13: PC1 (10.0.0.2/25), PC3 (10.0.0.3/25)
- VLAN 24: PC2 (10.0.0.130/25), PC4 (10.0.0.131/25)
- Router: R1 performs Inter-VLAN routing via subinterfaces.
- Switches: SW1 (core) and SV (access).

------------------------------------------------------------
üîç Issues Found & Fixes

[1] Router (R1)
Issue:
  Subinterface Gig0/0.13 was misconfigured (wrong IP or missing encapsulation).
Fix:
  interface GigabitEthernet0/0.13
   encapsulation dot1Q 13
   ip address 10.0.0.1 255.255.255.128

[2] Switch SV (Access Switch)
Issue:
  Trunk port between SW1 and SV was not configured correctly.
Fix:
  interface GigabitEthernet0/1
   switchport mode trunk
   switchport trunk allowed vlan 13,24

[3] PCs (End Devices)
Issue:
  Some PCs had incorrect default gateways.
Fix:
  VLAN 13 PCs ‚Üí Gateway 10.0.0.1
  VLAN 24 PCs ‚Üí Gateway 10.0.0.129
